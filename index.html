An Introduction To The
Sri Lankan Wildlife

Sri Lanka, a tropical island nation located in South Asia, is renowned for its rich and diverse.
The country's unique geographical features, including dense rainforests, highlands, and coastal areas,
contribute to its remarkable biodiversity. Sri Lanka is considered a biodiversity hotspot, meaning it harbors a high number of species that are found nowhere else on Earth.
